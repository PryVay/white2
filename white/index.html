1. Steps to work with us
Begin by scheduling a consultation with our agency where we delve into your vision, preferences, and requirements for the project. We'll discuss your aesthetic preferences, functional needs, and budgetary considerations to ensure a thorough understanding of your goals.
2. Design Concept Development
After the initial consultation, our team will develop design concepts tailored to your specifications. Through mood boards, sketches, and 3D visualizations, we'll present you with various design options that align with your vision. We'll collaborate closely with you, refining the concepts until we achieve a design direction that resonates with your vision.
3. Detailed Planning and Execution
Once the design concept is approved, we move on to the detailed planning phase. This involves selecting materials, furnishings, fixtures, and accessories that bring the design to life. Our team meticulously plans every aspect of the project, ensuring seamless coordination and execution from start to finish.
4. Project Management
With the plans finalized, we proceed to implement the design, managing every aspect of the project to ensure it stays on schedule and within budget. From coordinating contractors and overseeing installations to quality control checks, we handle all aspects of the project with precision and professionalism, keeping you informed and involved every step of the way.
Cost calculation and payment
Our process of cost estimation and payment proceeds in several stages to ensure transparency and client comfort. Initially, our specialists conduct a detailed assessment of all project aspects, considering the scope of work, selected materials, and potential additional services. Based on this assessment, a detailed estimate is prepared, breaking down all expenses clearly.
After presenting the estimate, we discuss and approve the final project budget with the client, taking into account all their preferences and capabilities.Subsequently, we offer flexible payment terms, dividing the total sum into several stages according to the progress of work. Clients can choose a payment schedule that suits them best, ensuring comfortable and secure financial management throughout the project.
